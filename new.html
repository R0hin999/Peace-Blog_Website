What having a peaceful mind means to me and how to achieve it

To most people peace refers to the absence of violence. But it means that you have enough understanding to keep yourself strong and calm in the face of stress or any problem . In Today's World Stress is everywhere whether it comes from social media apps , work , school or college , relationships . We are constantly bombarded with information and demands . This constant flow of stimuli makes it hard to focus on anything else . And when we Don't make enough time to relax and rest it often results in burnout and mental exhaustion .

Everyone knows that stress is not good ; it leads to anxiety and other health issues . Being busy is good but having spare time to slow down and enjoy life can provide imense relief to mind and can recover mental fatigue . Having inner peace is as important as keeping you body fit and soul healthy . We all go through good and bad times ; some we cherish , some we regret .

We spend most of our time thinking about past and future . We keep ruminating when we have done some mistake or due to some traumatic experience we had in past . We also judge ourselves and make assumptions how people pereceive us . During this time whatever thoughts comes to our mind we believe its true and this misleads our perception .

A person with peaceful mind is happier , more optimistic and deals with situations calmly . When there is inner peace stress , self doubts , negative thoughts fades away. It seems harder to attain peace as we are surrounded by negative news , views and being most of times on phone screens has made it all worse . It is the high time for everyone to learn the art of being in peace .

" The Pursuit of inner peace is more important then search for happiness or success . Happiness is but a fleeting emotion , and success can be taken from us just as it can be gained . But true inner peace is permanent "

Chris Shea

You might think you can only be in peace when you don't have problems to deal with or after achieving some goals , but thats not true . Infact , its the other way around .And it is not necessary to be a buddhist monk or sadhu in order to be in peace. But if we will spend our endless time in thinking about things we dont like about ourselves and past regrets , we will never feel peaceful. Nobody is perfect , removing all expectations will let you appreciate your life as is .when you learn to accept your flaws instead of magnifying them , you create a healthy and peaceful environment for yourself . No doubt its not that easy to have inner peace , but once you get hang of it , it will be a piece of cake . So here are some tips or was to achieve or maintain peaceful mind .
So here are few ways to become a person of peace

1.Meditation

Meditation is one of my favourite and best way to maintain peaceful mind . It helps in reducing stress and provides some new or different perspective on thoughts . It improves focus , concentration and memory . One must practice meditation for at least 5-10 minutes daily.There are various types of meditation and there isn't any right or wrong way to meditate.Mindfulness meditation is the most effective to achieve peace .

2.Journaling

Journaling can help you declutter your mind and reduce stress . Writing about thoughts that are bothering you or feelings can help you clarify your mind . Moreover it also improves your problem solving skills . To just write a journal you just need a paper or diary and pen . So start journaling now it will help you to learn more about yourself too .

3.Exercise

Exercise is known to be a stress buster. Being active can boost chemicals in your brain to enhance your mood. A simple walk in the garden can promote peace of mind. Exercise can reduce your depression, bad mood, stress, and laziness. Exercise helps you to live a healthy life .

4.Music

Music is a powerful tool for mood regulation and stress. It can lift you up when you're low and calm you down when you're anxious.The best music for stress reduction is the kind that works for you. You can also use music for motivation when you're feeling low . You can listen to music while studying , painting , while doing workout , cooking and whenever you want to ; It just makes life more fun enjoyable .

5.Hobby

Find yourself a hobby which is different from your daily work so that it can give you peace . For me its painting it relaxes my mind and reduces my stress . Whenever i go for Vacations i bring a sketchbook with me so that i can do sketching . The process of making any kind of artwork is itself such therapeutic and wonderful thing . It requires creative and critical thinking skills to decide what to draw or sketch , making concept art , deciding the colors to use , blending the colors . It also sharpens the mind through conceptual visualization and implementation and boosts memory skills . If you dont want to make artworks in traditonal style as it takes lots of efforts so you can make artworks digitally with the help of softwares it just makes process easy and save lots of time and money . I personally use Adobe Photoshop for making digital art . Digital Art is beginner friendly too so you can make really wonderful artworks with just minimum efforts even if you do mistakes in it you can just simply undo . So find a hobby that gives you inner peace .
